Summary

My passion for game design is from a combination of creativity and problem-solving skills. Game design allows me to blend my artisity with technical proficiency, creating immersive designs for players/clients to find the designs appealing.

Key Skills

My key skills for becoming a potential game designer or designer in general would be:

  • Creativity, being able to generate original ideas for game mechanics, characters, storyline. I am also able to emphatize with any client that gives me an idea on what I'd like to create.
  • Storyboarding and Level Design, visualizing game enivronments and structuring gameplay experiences through storytelling and level design is crucial for creating engaging gameplay.
  • User Experience (UX) Design, designing gameplay experiences that are intutive, immersive and enjoyable for players through user-crentered design principles.
  • Critial Thinking, evaluating game mechanics, player/client feedback and market trends to make informed designs and iterate on game concepts effectively.
  • Communication, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams, converying ideas clearly, and providing constructive feedback throughout the development process.
  • Testing and Iteration, conducting playtests, gathering feedback and iterating on game design elements to refine gameplay mechanics and improve the overall user experience.
  • Time Management, meeting project deadlines and managing priorities in a dynamic environment improved my organizational skills.
